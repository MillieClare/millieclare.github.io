CrossFit
I like to lift heavy things.

I've been training at a CrossFit gym since early 2018. I enjoy the community aspect, as well as the wide variety of classes on offer. It's a long (usually daily) struggle, but no matter how hard the WOD is, I walk (or limp!) home lighter and happier, with a bunch of cool party tricks. The assault bike however, is my mortal enemy, and the flexibility classes are my best friends.

Make-Up
I like to paint with colour.

My secret passion is make-up: creating art using the face as a canvas. We live in an age where our lives are a highlight reel posted online. While this does have it's negatives, it's also opened up a world of inspiration, with people sharing their art across various social platforms. I am constantly inspired by the works of others and regularly take to my own face to create my own art. I think that make-up is liberating: it gives the freedom to be confident, and express who you are. It brings people together, whether it's over a highlight that can be seen from space, or the perfect transition shade for the crease.

Traveling
I like to see new things.

I've been fortunate enough to explore some awesome places and see a lot of really cool things. I love chasing the sun! Next on my wish list is America, and I'd love to get to know more of Asia.

I think my favourite thing about it all is the food. And the animals.

Twitch.
I like to shoot things. But usually I miss.

I've been part of the Twitch streaming community for just under a year now. I enjoy a variety of games and interacting with my community. I've played video games for a very long time, and it's one of my favourite ways to wind down.

Sometimes I do some awesome things, but more often than not things don't go exactly to plan...
