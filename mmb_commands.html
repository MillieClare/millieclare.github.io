Miniminibot_ Commands

Miniminibot_


Miniminibot_ (or MMB for short) is my bot that you will see in my channel while I am streaming. She is super friendly - say hello to her! In general, most of the minigames that you play or any time you are using currency in stream, that is MMB using the StreamLabs client, while everything else is code that I have written myself. Below is a full list of commands, and where they come from.

Occasionally there will be temporary commands in the stream if I'm involved in any streaming events, and they may not always be updated on here. All commands on this page are permanent.

If you have any suggestions for MMB, please feel free to drop me a line - MMB is just a baby right now, but I have big plans for her future!


Commands from StreamLabs Chatbot that MMB uses

  • !points - how many points you have
  • !givepoints (moderator only)
  • !heist (amount) - start the bank robbery!
  • !challenge (username) - challenge a fellow viewer to a duel. Who will be the victor?

Commands written by me that MMB uses

  • !hi - say 'hi!' to MMB
  • !time - what time is in the U.K.
  • !uptime - how long I've been streaming for
  • !commands - a link to this page, just in case you forget any commands!
  • !sub
  • !follow
  • !hype
  • !lurk
  • !discord
  • !twitter
  • !so (moderator only)
