Here you'll find my completed projects and information about the tools I've used to get me to where I am now.

I like to learn through doing. My first stop on my reintroduction to programming was 'Human Resource Machine' from the Tomorrow Corporation, which I thoroughly enjoyed. Outside this, I have been using Codecademy and have so far completed their 'Learn HTML', 'Learn CSS', 'Introduction to JavaScript' and 'Make a Website' courses. I am currently part-way through 'Learn Git'. To support my studying, I have also been reading and practically working through 'A Smarter Way to Learn JavaScript' by Mark Myers to further my knowledge of key language and syntax, 'Eloquent JavaScript' by Marijn Haverbeke to develop my understanding of concepts, and 'The Non-Designer's Design Book' by Robin Williams, to make sure everything is aesthetically pleasing.
